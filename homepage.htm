Welcome to
The Qula Project

This project seeks to gather and analyze extensive data on the types of foods Kenyans buy from supermarkets and their nutritional values.

By employing data analytics and machine learning techniques, the project will identify prevalent dietary patterns and assess their nutritional implications. The findings will help understand consumer behavior, promote healthier eating choices, and potentially influence food policy and product offerings in supermarkets. The project aims to provide stakeholders, including health organizations, policymakers, and the food industry, with insights to foster a healthier national diet.
